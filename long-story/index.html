Rain Walking

A long-scrolling story experience.

It's raining

You just got off of work and missed the train. You decided it'd be better to camp out in town to wait for it to pass.

Same thoughts

You spot a dog across the street. You don't know if it belongs to someone, but it seems to have also been caught in the rain and is waiting by a cafe.

Crowds pass, no one comes.

Who does this dog belong to? Does it belong to anyone? Countless peopel walked by, glancing but never stopping. The rain doesn't help.

Well, the rain isn't going anywhere.

You run across the street and grab a chair near the dog. It looks at you nervously, but then calms when you give it a gentle pet. Looks like you won't have to wait alone. Maybe this dog won't have to be alone.
