Shoyu Salmon Poke

Prep Time: 10 min

Feeds: 2 people (or one if ur extra hungry)

Ingredients!

  • 1 pound Salmon, flash-frozen (Or preferred fish)
  • 1/4 Shoyu/Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 1 tsp Seasame Oil
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 Cup Sweet Onion
  • 3 Stalks Green Onion

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add your shoyu, oyster sauce, seasame oil, sugar, and salt.
  2. Whisk it all together to dissolve the salt and sugar into the marinade.
  3. Julienne the sweet onion and add it to your marinade.
  4. Thinly slice your green onion on a bias and add those into your marinade as well.
  5. Add your poke cubes to your marinade and mix it all together so each of the cubes are fully coated.
  6. Serve over rice, required.
  7. Eat right away or let it marinate overnight for richer flavor.
