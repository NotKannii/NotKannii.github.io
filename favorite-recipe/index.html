Crispy Salmon Bites

Quick snack with an addicting crunch! Air fry for the best convenience.

Ingredients!

  • 2 Salmon Filets
  • 1 Cup Japanese Bread Crumbs
  • 1/2 tbs salt
  • 1/2 tbs pepper
  • 1 tbs paprika
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • Avocado cooking spray
  • Half lemon
  • [Optional] Additional seasonings

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 350 degrees
  2. Cut salmon filets into medium sized cubes
  3. Season salmon cubes generously
  4. Coat salmon cubes with thin layer of olive oil
  5. Coat all sides of the cubes with Japanese Panko
  6. Place cubes into air fryer and spray top with avocado spray
  7. Fry for 8 mintues or until golden brown
  8. Plate and serve!
