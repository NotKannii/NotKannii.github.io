Crispy Salmon Bites

As seen in my kitchen...

Prep Time: 10 min | Cook Time: 30 min | Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 2 Salmon Filets
  • 1 Cup Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 1/2 tbs salt
  • 1/2 tbs pepper
  • 1 tbs paprika
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • Avocado cooking spray
  • Half lemon
  • [Optional] Garlic powder

Directions:

  1. Preheat air fryer to 350 degrees F.
  2. Cut salmon filets into medium sized cubes.
  3. Season salmon cubes generously with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  4. Coat salmon cubes with a thin layer of olive oil.
  5. Coat all sides of the cubes with Japanese Panko.
  6. Place cubes into air fryer and spray top with avocado spray.
  7. Fry for 8 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Plate, squeeze fresh lemon over the top, and serve!
