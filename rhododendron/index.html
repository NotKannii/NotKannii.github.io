Pacific Rhododendron

Rhododendron Macrophyllum

Also known as the California rose bay, California rhododendron, coast rhododendron or big leaf rhododendron.

Sizing

The Pacific Rhododendron grows between 6 to 12 feet tall, but can reach up to 20 feet in the best conditions.

Geographic Range

It is found along the Pacific Coast, from British Columbia through Washington and Oregon to Northern California.

Climate

This plant thrives in mild, coastal climates with moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.
